About Sib Consultants

SIB Consulting is an affiliate of Sennex Consultants. Established in 2001, Sennex is a leading strategic planning, interior architecture and construction firm, with extensive experience on high profile projects across the region and worldwide. Headquartered out of Singapore, Sennex has operating entities in Hong Kong & Malaysia, with collaborative partners in Japan, PRC, Korea, Thailand, Taiwan, Indonesia, Australia and the United Arab Emirates. Sennex is a strategic alliance partner of the internationally renowned US firm IA Interior Architects, who have offices throughout the Unites States & Europe.

SIB was established in 2008, in Dubai. SIB is a firm of highly creative and natural problem solvers. We have the ability to understand the business of our clients and their future business challenges, which allows us to add value to the project programming process. This saves time and ensures a degree of future proofing. With a working relationship that has existed for over 8 years, the principals of SIB, Daniel Bunce and Keith Edwards, together with their team, have the experience and practical expertise to deliver complex, high quality projects - on time and to budget.

OUR DIRECTORS

Daniel Bunce has many years of experience within Food & Beverage operations, as well as new start up concepts, design, build, and brand growth. After leaving Sharaf Group, where he was CEO for Sharaf Hospitality, he joined SIB Consulting, as a managing partner. Daniel has over 16 years’ experience in delivering projects, and with his sound background in this industry and knowledge of the Dubai hospitality sector. He brings a wealth of experience to the Group Worldwide.

Keith Edwards has been in the industry for over 20 years. He has vast experience in building commercial, retail and F & B projects throughout Asia, the Middle East, the United States and the United Kingdom. Keith has worked with the founder of Sennex, Nick Jones, since 1998 and has been delivering projects in Dubai since 2008. With strong knowledge of all sectors, Keith has delivered for some of the biggest names in banking and fortune five hundred companies, across the globe.
